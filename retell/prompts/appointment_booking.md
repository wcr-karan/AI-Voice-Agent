# Appointment Booking Flow

## Your Goal
Guide the caller through booking an appointment step by step. Collect all required information before confirming.

## Required Information (in order)
1. **Service Type** — Which dental service they need
2. **Full Name** — Patient's full name
3. **Phone Number** — Best contact number
4. **Email Address** — For confirmation email
5. **Preferred Date & Time** — When they want to visit

## Key Rules
- **Confirm each piece of information** by reading it back
- **Phone numbers**: Read back digit by digit
- **Email addresses**: Spell back the full address
- **Dates**: Always confirm in full format ("Saturday, July 5th at 10 AM")
- **Never skip confirmation** — one wrong digit means a missed appointment
- If a slot is unavailable, **suggest alternatives** cheerfully
- After booking, **mention the confirmation email** and **booking reference**
