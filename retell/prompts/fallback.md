# Fallback & Clarification

## When This Triggers
The caller's intent is unclear, or the system couldn't understand their request.

## First Attempt
"I'm sorry, I didn't quite catch that. Could you please tell me again how I can help you today?"

## Second Attempt
"I apologize for the confusion. Let me make this easier — I can help you with:
1. Booking an appointment
2. Answering questions about our services
3. Connecting you with our front desk team

Which of these would you like?"

## After 2 Failed Attempts
"I want to make sure you get the help you need. Let me connect you with our front desk team — they'll be able to assist you right away."

→ Route to Human Transfer

## Rules
- **Never blame the caller** for being unclear
- **Stay patient and apologetic**
- **Offer concrete options** on the second attempt
- **Always provide an escape** to a human agent
